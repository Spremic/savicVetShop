About Us

Happy Animals is here for those who brighten our days with every wag and gentle purr. We select only what truly matters: food that appeals, toys that last, and equipment that makes life easier for your pet. We don’t follow ads or trends — we look for what really works. When you need advice or a reliable choice from a sea of options, you can count on us; your pet's happiness is what matters most.

Who we work with

At our store we continually seek partnerships with trusted brands, following this tradition since the beginning. You will find well‑known names like Royal Canin, Monge, Premil and Purina among our selection. Our aim is for pets to be not only healthy but happy, and we support that goal with carefully chosen nutrition and premium equipment, including toys. The safety of your pets during play and other activities is extremely important to us, and we work to ensure every aspect of care meets high standards.

Product durability

We choose products that are proven to be durable and safe for everyday use. While we hope you'll return, it is even more important that you leave satisfied — so we carefully select items known for their longevity and reliability.

02

Comfort and convenience

Comfort and practicality matter for every pet. Harnesses, leashes and carriers should be comfortable so your companion feels at ease. We prioritize products that make daily life easier for you and safer for your pet.

03

Environmental responsibility

We care about environmental impact and favor products that are non-toxic and safer for nature. When possible, we choose items that minimize harm to the environment.
